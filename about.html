About Us

A Madagascar-based international trading house engaged in the export and import of agro commodities and merchandise across global markets.

Company Overview

Who We Are

Mada Overseas was established in 2006 as a single ownership firm headquartered in Antananarivo, Madagascar. The Company is engaged in the business of Trading and Export of Commodities, with core exports consisting of Agro Based Commodities.

We are engaged in International Trading and Domestic Trading activities. Main activities under International Export include Direct Export, Indirect Exports and Imports of various goods and merchandise. Domestically, we deal in sales and purchase of Commodities.

Over the years the Company has developed exports of various agro based commodities including Spices, Lentils, Vanilla, Sugar, Wheat Flour, Rice, Soya Bean Meal, Chick Peas, Chrome Ore, Copper Ore and more.

Main Object

To carry on business globally as Traders, Dealers, Exporters, Importers, Buyers, Sellers, Merchants, Indenters, Commission Agents, Brokers, Buying and Selling Agents, Factors, Distributors, and Stockists in all kinds of products and articles of merchandise — and to act as manufacturers representatives and to set up import and export houses for all types of products required or ordered by customers.
